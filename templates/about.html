Our Story

TruckkPro Consultancy Firm was founded with one mission — to solve the real challenges facing truck owners, drivers, and Saccos in Kenya.

With 9 years of hands-on experience in the trucking industry, we have grown into a trusted partner for transport businesses across the country. We understand the roads, the regulations, and the people behind the wheel.

From KeNHA permit processing to fleet management and driver training, every solution we offer is built from real industry experience — not theory.
